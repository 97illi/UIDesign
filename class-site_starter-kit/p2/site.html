What is a Makjang?


Makjang is a genre of Korean drama. The word makjang can be translated into “taking things too far.

A drama is usually considered a makjang if it has out-of-this-world, greatly exaggerated, and overly dramatic plots and characterizations. They defy norms and conventional moral standards.

A Mexican telenovella is an equivalent of a Korean Makjang drama.
